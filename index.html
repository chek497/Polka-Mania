What is Polka?

Polka refers to an originally Czech dance and genre of dance music that has become familiar throughout the Americas and Europe.

Polka is typically a genre entertained by folk musicians and folk bands.

There are several styles of Polka, including:

  • Polish-style Polka with roots in Chicago
  • North American Slovenian-style Polka
  • The Dutchmen-style Polka from the American Midwest
  • Conjunto-style Polka originating from northern Mexico and Texas

As you can see, Polka has a very rich and diverse history across the world.
